About UKUAT

Urban Agtech is a growing sector in the UK. Utilising this technology in urban and peri-urban areas could help the country economically, in meeting its sustainability goals, and in enhancing resilience and nutritional security.
The goal of UKUAT is to increase recognition of the Urban Agtech sector and the potential solutions it offers for this country. To that end, it brings together key sector players, of all types, to collaborate and so advance their common interests. Areas of collaboration to include (but not necessarily be limited to): Policy & Advocacy Events Education & Outreach Research & Development Publicity & Promotion Access funding through consortiums
